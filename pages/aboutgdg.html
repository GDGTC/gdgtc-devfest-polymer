Hosting Google and Open Source Development Events in the greater Washington DC


Google Developer Group Washington DC (GDG-DC) is a developer group that is passionate about discussing, discovering, and distributing Google technologies. GDG-DC welcomes it's members from Washington DC, Northern Virginia, and Maryland.

Everything from the Android, App Engine, and Google Chrome platforms, to product APIs like the Maps API, YouTube API and Google Calendar API.

Started as National Capital Area Google Tech User Group in April 2010, we have grown up to 1000+ members on Meetup and have conducted 25+ events so far.
