Android Wear Hackathon

Did we mention we also have a Wear hackathon in the program? That’s right, we’ll kick off the afternoon with a presentation on Android Wear & Glass design guidelines and then we’ll get right into a mini-hackathon lead by Josh Wilson and David Hodge from Glass + Wear DC.

At the end of the day a panel will judge the best hacks and we will award a Moto 360 and Google Play gift cards to the winning team! We’ll also give away a Remotte developer edition among hackathon participants.

In the meanwhile, if you have any questions, you can reach us at organizer@devfestdc.org
